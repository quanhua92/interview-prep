# Resume Structure — Practice Checklist

Use this checklist to build a clear, ATS-friendly resume that highlights your impact.
Target a single-page resume for under 10 years of experience, or two pages for senior roles.

## Drafting Phase
- [ ] List all roles in reverse chronological order with dates (month/year)
- [ ] Write 3-5 bullet points per role starting with strong action verbs
- [ ] Include at least one quantified metric per role (e.g., "reduced latency by 40%")
- [ ] Add a concise Professional Summary (2-3 sentences) tailored to your target role
- [ ] List relevant skills, tools, and technologies grouped by category

## Refinement Phase
- [ ] Run your resume through an ATS scanner or paste into a plain text editor to check formatting
- [ ] Remove any bullet that describes responsibility rather than achievement
- [ ] Check that bullet points follow the "X to Y to Z" framework: Action + What You Did + Result
- [ ] Check your font choice — use a clean, standard font (e.g., Calibri, Garamond, Helvetica) at 10-11pt for body text
- [ ] Save and export your resume as PDF to preserve formatting — never send a .docx unless specifically requested
- [ ] Check your Professional Summary against the good/bad examples: avoid generic adjectives ("passionate," "hardworking") and include specific, quantified details
- [ ] Check for over-designed elements (colors, graphics, photos, unusual layouts) that may break ATS parsers
- [ ] Trim each bullet to a single line (no wrapping) where possible
- [ ] Have a peer or mentor review for clarity and impact

## Practice Phase
- [ ] Read your Professional Summary aloud — can you deliver it in under 30 seconds?
- [ ] Walk through each role verbally in 60 seconds without looking at the resume
- [ ] Time a full resume walkthrough at 3-5 minutes total
