# Resume & Career

Preparation for resume optimization and career narrative in interviews. Each topic has:

- **discussion.md** — Best practices, structure/framework, good vs bad examples, common mistakes
- **checklist.md** — Drafting phase, refinement phase, practice phase, and practice log

## Topics

| Topic | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| [Resume Structure](resume_structure/) | One-page format, sections, bullet writing |
| [Elevator Pitch](elevator_pitch/) | 30-60 second self-introduction |
| [Career Narrative](career_narrative/) | Connecting your experience into a coherent story |
| [Explaining Gaps](explaining_gaps/) | Employment gaps, career changes, layoffs |
| [Portfolio Showcase](portfolio_showcase/) | Presenting projects, GitHub, writing samples |
| [Questions for Interviewer](questions_for_interviewer/) | Strategic questions to ask at the end of interviews |

## How to Practice

1. Read the **discussion.md** for structure guidance and examples
2. Open the **checklist.md** and work through drafting, refinement, and practice phases
3. Practice your elevator pitch and career narrative out loud (record yourself)
4. Time each response — aim for the target duration
5. Each topic needs 2-3 rounds of practice with refinement
